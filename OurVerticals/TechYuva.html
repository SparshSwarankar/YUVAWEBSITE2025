Our Vertical

Tech YUVA

Fostering innovation and technological development among the youth.

Introduction & Vision

Tech YUVA is an initiative aimed at fostering innovation and technological development among youth. The platform offers resources, training, and opportunities for young minds to explore emerging technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain, and IoT.

Vision: To inspire and equip the next generation of innovators and technologists who can drive India’s future in technology and digital transformation.

Objectives

  • To create awareness about emerging technologies.
  • To provide a hands-on learning experience through workshops and labs.
  • Encourage innovation and entrepreneurship among tech-savvy youth.
  • Facilitate connections between industry leaders and young technologists.

Working Model

Tech YUVA hosts regular tech bootcamps, coding competitions, and innovation labs. The initiative works closely with tech startups, academic institutions, and government bodies to identify technological gaps and provide young innovators with the tools to bridge them.

Major Events

  • Hackathons and Coding Competitions: National-level events where students and young professionals compete to solve real-world problems using technology.
  • Technology Bootcamps: Short-term intensive courses on topics such as AI, Cloud Computing, and Cybersecurity.
  • Innovation Showcases: Opportunities for students to present their projects to potential investors and industry leaders.
