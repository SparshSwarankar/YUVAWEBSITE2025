Media Manthan

Fostering patriotism and responsibility in the fourth democratic pillar.

Introduction & Vision

Media Manthan stands as a collective of dynamic media professionals and mass communication students dedicated to fostering patriotism, upholding human values, and strengthening the fourth democratic pillar through cultural and social responsibility.

Vision: "In its inception, Media Manthan envisions itself as a student-based organization, placing a strong emphasis on the ethos of 'India First' among media students. The organization aims to harness the creative energy of these students, directing it towards impactful projects that address social issues and contribute positively to the community. Looking ahead, Media Manthan aspires to make significant contributions to the media landscape, fostering social discourse, promoting inclusivity, and inspiring positive change. Through its media content, Media Manthan seeks to inspire a profound sense of pride and patriotism, highlighting Bharat's achievements, history, and valuable contributions to the world."

Objectives

  • "Establish a media student forum in Delhi for organizing academic, cultural, social, and sports activities."
  • "Empower media students through learning, leadership, and exploration of interests, enhancing organizational skills."
  • “Create a shared space for media students to converse and exchange ideas.”
  • "Facilitate student understanding, connections, and empathy for successful interaction with the media industry and the global community."
  • Encourage individual character development leading to social awakening.
  • "Shift media students' thinking from Western thought to Indian Knowledge System (IKS) rooted in classical literature and wisdom from seers, saints,and sages."

Working Model

Media Manthan functions as a Student based Organisation and a collaborative forum. Regular webinars, expert talks, interviews with key stakeholders, and detailed publications form the core of its working structure. The team curates insightful content, including interviews with Media Professionals and policy experts, and also produces audio-visual materials like podcasts, short documentaries, and articles.

Major Events

  • Indraprastha Media Samvaad: An event bringing together international experts on Media and Journalism.
  • Workshops on Investigative Journalism: Training sessions for budding journalists.
  • Media Experts’ Panel Discussions: Insightful discussions on national security issues.
