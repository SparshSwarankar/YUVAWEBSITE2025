Our Vertical

Medico YUVA

Enhancing healthcare education and raising public health awareness.

Introduction & Vision

Medico YUVA focuses on enhancing healthcare education, raising awareness about public health issues, and offering practical medical training. It targets young medical professionals, providing them with the latest insights and resources to improve healthcare delivery.

Vision: To revolutionize healthcare education and training through digital platforms, ensuring that every healthcare professional has access to the knowledge and tools needed to improve patient care.

Objectives

  • To improve access to updated medical education and resources.
  • Encourage awareness about preventive health care and medical advancements.
  • Provide a platform for young healthcare professionals to collaborate and share knowledge.
  • Facilitate medical research and innovation among youth.

Working Model

Medico YUVA operates through webinars, virtual workshops, and expert sessions. It brings together medical students, doctors, and healthcare professionals to discuss advancements in medical research, public health policies, and healthcare technology.

Major Events

  • Healthcare Webinars: Monthly webinars on topics like preventive healthcare, mental health, and medical technology.
  • Medical Training Camps: Virtual and in-person camps for hands-on training in first-aid, diagnostics, and treatment.
  • Public Health Awareness Drives: Campaigns to spread awareness about common health issues such as diabetes, hypertension, and mental well-being.
