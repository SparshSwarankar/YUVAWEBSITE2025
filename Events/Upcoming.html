Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our events

How do I register for an event?

Simply click on any event card to view details and find the registration button. You can register directly through our platform or external links provided.

Can I attend events online?

Many of our events offer both in-person and online participation. Check the event details for the specific mode of participation available.

Is there a registration fee?

Most YUVA events are free to attend. Some specialized workshops or premium events may have a nominal fee. Check individual event details for pricing information.

How can I get involved as a volunteer?

We love volunteers! Visit our volunteer page to learn about opportunities and submit your interest. You can also reach out to us directly at events@yuva.org.

What should I bring to an event?

Bring a valid ID, notebook, and pen. For outdoor events, wear comfortable clothing and bring water. Specific requirements are mentioned in individual event details.
