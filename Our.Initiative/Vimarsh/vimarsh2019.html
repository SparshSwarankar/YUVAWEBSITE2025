About Vimarsh

Vimarshe 2019 was a landmark student conference held at the University of Delhi. The event, themed "Awakened Bharat," was notable for blending high-level academic discussions with a pioneering commitment to sustainability (fully plastic-free) and gender equality (achieving 50% female attendance). It successfully set a new benchmark for intellectually rigorous and socially conscious events.
