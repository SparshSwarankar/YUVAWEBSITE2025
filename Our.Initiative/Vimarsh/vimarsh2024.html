About Vimarsh

Vimarsh 2024, organized by YUVA, brought together diverse voices to discuss key themes including national security, cultural heritage, gender, environment, economy, and holistic development in India. Panel sessions featured distinguished speakers who discussed internal and external threats to India, challenges in the Northeast, cyber security, and the significance of unity and Ekatma Drishti in sustaining the nation. The cultural sessions explored the decolonization of Indian minds, the role of temples, and the impact of cinema on national identity.
